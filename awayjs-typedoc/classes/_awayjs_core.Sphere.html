union

  • union(toUnion, target?): Sphere

  • Adds two spheres together to create a new Sphere object, by filling in the horizontal, vertical and longitudinal space between the two spheres.

    <p><b>Note:</b> The <code>union()</code> method ignores spheres with <code>0</code> as the height, width or depth value, such as: <code>var box2:Sphere = new Sphere(300,300,300,50,50,0);</code></p>

    Parameters

    • toUnion: Sphere

      A Sphere object to add to this Sphere object.

    • target: Sphere = null

    Returns Sphere

    A new Sphere object that is the union of the two spheres.
